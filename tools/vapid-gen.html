VAPID Key Generator

Generiruyet P-256 keypair dlya Web Push. Vsyo proiskhodit lokal'no v brauzere cherez Web Crypto API — klyuchi NE otpravlyayutsya nikuda.

Vazhno: sokhrani Private Key v nadyozhnom meste. On dayot pravo otpravlyat' push vsem podpisannym ustroystvam. Yesli uteryayesh' — pridyotsya peregenerirovat' (vse podpiski pereobnovyatsya).

Chto delat' dal'she

  1. Public key poydyot v kod PWA (fayl app.js, peremennaya VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY)
  2. Private key poydyot v Cloudflare Worker kak secret (cherez dashboard ili wrangler secret put VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY). Nikogda ne commit' v git.
  3. Subject \u2014 tvoy email v formate mailto:.... Tozhe v Worker settings kak VAPID_SUBJECT.
